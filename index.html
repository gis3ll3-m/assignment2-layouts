Welcome to UNCG Eats!

Are you on the look out for tasty food near campus? This blog highlights some of the best spots to catch a bite, from study-worthy cafe spots, to sushi and mexican cuisine.

We've gathered up some of the best local spots to make eating easy and simple. After visiting these places, you'll know where to grab a coffee, a quick bite, or meal! Each resturant below features what to try and why these spots are a favorite!

Tate Street Coffee

Located on Tate Street, this coffee shop is perfect for studying, breakfast, or for casual meetups!

Tate St Coffee is open daily from 6:30AM - 6PM

Iced Coffee

Create a personalized coffee with all the available syrups! Perfect for mid-day pick me up!

Bagel Sandwich

Sausage egg and cheese bagel, perfect for breakfast!

Muffins

Freshly made muffins (of different flavors) to pair with with any drink!

Sushi Republic

Located across Tate St Coffee, this sushi house serves the best specialty sushi rolls and unique desserts.

Sushi Republic is open Mon - Fri: 11:30AM - 9:30PM. Sat: 5PM - 10PM. Closed on Sundays

Spicy Salmon Sushi

Crab meat, avocado, cucumber, masago, topped with spicy salmon, eel sauce and spicy mayo.

Stir Fry Noodles

Udon noodles stir fried together with vegetables.

Tempura Bomb

Strawberry ice cream wrapped in sponge cake & tempura topped with chocolate sauce and whipped cream.

Crazy Mexico

Located right next to Tate Street Coffee, this Mexican resturant serves the best authentic mexican food!

Crazy Mexico is open Mon - Sat: 11AM-10PM. Closed on Sundays

Shrimp Fajitas

A popular dish that combines shrimp with mixed vegetables!

Camarones a La Diabla

Shrimp covered in a spicy chile pepper sauce!

Enchiladas

Corn tortilla filled with ground beef, topped with a special red sauce, lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, and sour cream!

These Tate Street spots currently do NOT take Flex dollars.
